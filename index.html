Empathy

Design Thinking is about designing for a user. One must empathise and find the user's real needs.

Define

It is crucial to identify a clear problem that your user wants solved.

Ideate

Start with quantity, but one must refine their ideas in a manner that considers the user's needs and constraints.

Iterate

Prototyping, developing, and feedback-based improvements are important for a successful user-focused product.

Deliver

A good product won't be of much use if nobody knows about it. Present it to your audience in an engaging way that draws interest to your product.

Evolve

Reflect and learn. What went well, what didn't? How do I fine-tune processes to achieve better results? How should I move forward from now?
